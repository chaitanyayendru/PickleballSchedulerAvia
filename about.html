Pickleball Scheduler

How it works

Accounts & groups

Sign up with an email and password. After logging in, you can either create a group (you become the captain) or request to join an existing one. Groups can have up to 6 members.

Each captain runs one group; group members can book on its behalf once it has at least 4 people.

Booking rules

  • Slots are 1 hour long. The court is open 24 hours.
  • A group needs at least 4 members before it can book.
  • Same hour, same group: at most twice per week (e.g. 6–7 PM Monday and 6–7 PM Wednesday, but not also Friday).
  • At most 16 bookings per week per group.
  • Multiple bookings per day are fine, as long as the weekly cap holds.

Extending into the next hour

If you're already on the court and want to keep playing, you can extend into the next hour once your current slot reaches its halfway point — provided the next hour is still free.

Extensions count toward your 16/week total, but they are exempt from the same-hour cap.

Requesting another group's slot

If another group is booked into a slot you want, you can send a polite swap request from the schedule. The other group sees it in their dashboard and either accepts (freeing the slot for you to book) or declines.

When email is configured, the requesting group also sends a note to the slot owner's leader email.

Cancellations

Your group can cancel its own bookings at any time. Cancelled slots immediately become available to everyone.

Privacy

All bookings are visible to everyone — that's the point. Leader email addresses are visible only to the system and used for swap-request notifications.
